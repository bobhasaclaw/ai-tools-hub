Overview

ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is the leading AI chatbot in 2026. Powered by GPT-5.3, GPT-5, GPT-4.1 family, it offers unparalleled conversational capabilities, coding assistance, and creative writing. With over 200 million weekly active users, ChatGPT dominates the AI assistant market.

Key Features

ChatGPT in 2026 includes the GPT-5.3 family: GPT-5.3 Codex for advanced coding, GPT-5.3 Codex-Spark for ultra-fast coding, and the flagship GPT-5 for general use. It features Advanced Voice Mode, Canvas for collaborative writing, and seamless integration with DALL-E 3 for image generation.

Pricing

ChatGPT Free offers basic access with usage limits. ChatGPT Go around $8/month gives increased limits. ChatGPT Plus $20/month for priority access and advanced features. ChatGPT Pro $200/month for highest access levels. Team plans around $25/user/month. API pricing varies: gpt-5.2 is $1.75 input/$14 output per 1M tokens.
