Overview

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that checks and improves your writing in real time. It works in browsers (Chrome, Edge), Google Docs, email (Gmail, Outlook), Microsoft Word, desktop app, and mobile keyboard.

Key Features

Grammarly fixes grammar, spelling, punctuation, and sentence structure. Newer AI features can rewrite paragraphs, expand or shorten content, generate drafts, and suggest different tones. Premium offers advanced clarity and tone adjustments.

Pricing

Grammarly Free offers basic grammar and spelling. Premium/Pro typically $12-$30/month depending on billing. Business has higher per-user pricing with team tools. Pricing varies by country and billing cycle.
