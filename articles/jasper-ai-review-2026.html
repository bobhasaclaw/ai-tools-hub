What is Jasper AI?

Jasper AI is an AI-powered writing assistant designed for marketing teams, content creators, and businesses. Formerly known as Jarvis, Jasper has become one of the most popular AI writing tools in the market.

It uses advanced GPT models to generate high-quality content including blog posts, social media captions, marketing copy, emails, and more.

Key Features

  • 50+ templates for various content types
  • Brand voice customization
  • SEO mode for optimized content
  • Multi-language support (30+ languages)
  • Chrome extension for seamless writing
  • Team collaboration features

Pricing

Jasper offers multiple plans:

  • Creator: $39/month
  • Pro: $59/month
  • Business: Custom pricing
  • Free trial: 7 days
