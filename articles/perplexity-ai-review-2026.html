What is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that combines web indexing with large language model capabilities to provide direct, conversational answers to user queries. Unlike traditional search engines that return a list of links, Perplexity synthesizes information from multiple sources to give you immediate, comprehensive responses.

Founded in 2022 by former Meta engineers, Perplexity has quickly become one of the most popular AI search tools available. The platform processes over 10 million queries daily and has raised significant funding to continue developing its search technology.

What sets Perplexity apart is its commitment to source transparency‚Äîevery answer includes citations to the original sources, allowing users to verify information and dive deeper into topics that interest them.

Key Features of Perplexity AI

1. Conversational Search

Perplexity understands context and can handle follow-up questions naturally. You can ask clarifying questions or refine your search without repeating context, making research more efficient and intuitive.

2. Source Citations

Every answer includes numbered citations linking to the original sources. This transparency sets Perplexity apart from other AI tools and makes it valuable for research, journalism, and academic work where source verification is essential.

3. Multiple AI Models

Perplexity offers access to various AI models, including:

  • GPT-4o - OpenAI's flagship model for general queries
  • Claude 3.5 Sonnet - Anthropic's model known for nuanced reasoning
  • Perplexity's own models - Optimized specifically for search

4. Thread Feature

Build persistent research threads to explore topics over time. Each thread maintains context, allowing you to return to previous searches and continue building on your research without losing momentum.

5. Focus Modes

Tailor your search results to specific needs:

  • Academic - Prioritizes peer-reviewed sources and scholarly articles
  • Writing - Optimized for content creators
  • Wolfram - Integrates computational knowledge
  • Reddit - Pulls from community discussions

6. Pro Search

The Pro version offers advanced capabilities including:

  • Unlimited searches
  • File uploads for analysis
  • API access for developers
  • Priority processing
