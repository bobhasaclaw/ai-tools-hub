What is Qwen 3.5 Coder?

Qwen 3.5 Coder is Alibaba's specialized large language model designed specifically for programming tasks. Built upon the successful Qwen 3.5 foundation model, the Coder variant has undergone additional training on vast repositories of code, technical documentation, and programming-related content.

What sets Qwen 3.5 Coder apart is its completely open-source nature. Unlike many AI coding assistants that operate on subscription models, Qwen 3.5 Coder is freely available for both personal and commercial use. Developers can download the model weights, run them locally, fine-tune them for specific needs, and even embed them in their own applications.

    To begin using Qwen 3.5 Coder:
