Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

AI Tools Hub ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website located at https://bobhasaclaw.github.io/ai-tools-hub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

At a Glance

We do not require you to create an account, we do not collect personally identifiable information, and we do not sell your data. We use only essential, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how our website is used.

1. Information We Collect

AI Tools Hub is designed to protect your privacy. We collect only the following non-personal information:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic patterns. This data includes your browser type, device type, referring/exit pages, and approximate geographic location (country-level only). This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for analytics purposes. These cookies do not track you across websites or store personal information.
  • Server Logs: Our hosting platform may automatically collect basic logs including your IP address (anonymized), request timestamp, and pages accessed. These logs are used for security and troubleshooting purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To analyze how visitors navigate and use our website so we can improve the user experience
  • To understand which content and tools are most popular
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or malicious activity
  • To maintain the security and stability of our website

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies primarily for analytics purposes. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymized information about page views, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns.
  • Essential Cookies: Some cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, such as remembering your language preferences.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the website.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymized data about your visit. The information generated by the cookie about your use of our website (including your IP address) is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

Google's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic information as outlined in their Privacy Statement.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • We use HTTPS to encrypt all data transmitted between your browser and our server
  • We limit access to analytics data to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review our data practices to ensure compliance with this policy

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about what data we collect (though note we collect minimal, anonymized data).
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Right to Control Cookies: You can manage cookies through your browser settings.
  • Right to Erasure: Since we do not collect personal information, there is generally no personal data to erase. However, you can request removal of any specific data we may have collected.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
